# Lead Research Strategy

How to research a target company or hiring manager before outreach. Follow these steps in order, using available MCPs.

## Research Steps

1. **Profile enrichment** via `{{PROFILE_ENRICHMENT}}`:
   - Hiring manager's LinkedIn profile: role, tenure, what they've built
   - Recent posts and activity — what do they care about?
   - Team members: who else is on the team, what's the team structure?

2. **Company research** via `{{WEBSITE_CRAWLING}}`:
   - What the company does, mission, recent product launches
   - Engineering blog or tech stack details
   - Recent news: funding, growth, pivots, new markets
   - Glassdoor/culture signals if publicly available
   - Open roles — is the role still actively posted?

3. **ICP matching** — cross-reference findings with `profile/icps.md`:
   - Which target company profile does this match?
   - What about your background aligns with their needs?
   - What specific value can you bring to their current challenges?

4. **Warm path check** via `{{FETCH_RELATIONSHIPS}}`:
   - Mutual connections at the company or who know the hiring manager
   - Former colleagues who work there now
   - Shared alumni networks, communities, or event attendance

## Output

Summarize research as:
- **Key findings**: 3-5 bullet points about the company and role
- **Personalization hooks**: specific details to reference (e.g., "your team's recent migration to Kubernetes," "the blog post about scaling to 10M users")
- **Value alignment**: how your background addresses their specific needs
- **Warm paths**: mutual connections or shared context
- **Recommended approach**: which template to use, what angle to lead with
