# Job Search Goals

## Primary Goal
Land a Head of Product or Senior PM role at a Series B-D developer tools or productivity company by May 2026.

## Target Role(s)
- Head of Product
- VP of Product (if team is small enough for a player-coach role)
- Senior Product Manager (at a company where this is the most senior IC role)

## Target Companies
- Series B-D SaaS companies (50-500 employees)
- Developer tools, productivity/collaboration, or platform infrastructure
- Product-led growth or strong PLG motion preferred
- Based in SF/NYC or remote-first

## Location Preferences
- Remote-first strongly preferred
- Open to hybrid in San Francisco or New York
- Willing to travel quarterly for offsites

## Salary Expectations & Compensation
- Base salary: $220K-$280K
- Total comp target: $350K-$450K (including equity)
- Equity: meaningful stake (0.1-0.5% at Series B-C stage)
- Standard benefits expected (health, 401k match, PTO)

## Success Metrics
- Networking conversations per week: 5
- Applications/outreaches sent per week: 8
- Reply rate on cold outreach: 30%+
- Phone screens per month: 6
- Formal interviews per month: 3
- Offers by end of search: 2-3

## Timeline

### February 2026 (Preparation)
- Finalize candidate brief and target company profiles
- Update LinkedIn profile and resume
- Build initial target list of 40 companies
- Begin warm intro outreach to network

### March 2026 (Active Outreach)
- 30+ outreach messages sent (mix of warm intros and cold)
- 10 networking conversations completed
- 4-6 phone screens scheduled
- Refine messaging based on early feedback

### April 2026 (Interview Phase)
- 6+ formal interview processes in progress
- 2-3 advancing to final rounds
- Begin compensation research for top candidates
- Practice case studies and product exercises

### May 2026 (Close)
- 2-3 offers in hand
- Negotiate compensation and start date
- Accept offer by end of May
- Target start date: June/July 2026

## Nice-to-Haves
- Company culture that values craft and user empathy over speed alone
- Direct report to CEO or co-founder
- Opportunity to build and lead a product team (hire 2-4 PMs)
- Exposure to AI/ML product challenges
- Strong engineering culture with high technical bar
- Inclusive, diverse leadership team
