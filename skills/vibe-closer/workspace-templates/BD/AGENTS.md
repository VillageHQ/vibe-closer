# BD & Partnerships Pipeline Workspace

This is a vibe-closer workspace for managing business development and partnership outreach pipelines.

## Quick Reference
- **Config**: `pipeline-config.md` — MCP providers, outreach channels, field mappings, and pipeline stages
- **MCP Hints**: `pipeline-mcp-hints.md` — concrete MCP tool call examples for configured providers
- **Goals**: `goals.md` — partnership targets and success metrics
- **Workflow**: `sequence-flow.md` — partnership outreach sequencing rules (stages are in config)
- **Company Brief**: `profile/company-brief.md` — your partnership value proposition
- **Partner Profiles**: `profile/ipp.md` — ideal partner profiles (IPPs)
- **Scheduling**: `profile/scheduling-links.md` — booking links and key URLs
- **Tone**: `messaging-guidelines/tone.md` — BD partnership tone and voice
- **Email Guidelines**: `messaging-guidelines/email-guidelines.md` — email best practices
- **Email Templates**: `messaging-guidelines/email-templates.md` — partnership outreach templates
- **LinkedIn**: `messaging-guidelines/linkedin-dm-guidelines.md` — LinkedIn partnership outreach
- **Discovery**: `partner_preferences/lead-discovery.md` — how to find and qualify partners
- **Partner Research**: `partner_preferences/research-lead.md` — how to research partners before outreach

- **View Config**: `.config/pipeline-view-config.js` — Supabase + CRM credentials and field mappings for the pipeline viewer (generated by `/update-skill` or `/onboard`)
- **Activity Viewer**: `activity-viewer.html` — browser-based pipeline viewer with leads and activity management

## Commands
- `/onboard` — Initial workspace configuration
- `/followup` — Process due partnerships and generate activities
- `/discover-leads` — Find new partner opportunities from conversations and emails
- `/learn` — Analyze partnership performance and improve workspace files
- `/view-pending-activity` — Open pipeline viewer to browse leads, review activities, approve outreach
- `/update-skill` — Update workspace to latest plugin version (syncs viewer, config, applies migrations)
- `/view-logs` — View command execution logs and outcomes

## Maintenance
- When files are added, removed, or renamed in this directory, update both CLAUDE.md and AGENTS.md to reflect the changes
