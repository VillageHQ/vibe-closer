# Partnership Email Templates

## Cold Outreach: Integration Partnership (Monitoring/Observability)

**Subject**: Surfacing {company_name} data in DevStack's service catalog

**Body**:
```
Hi {first_name},

I've been following {company_name}'s work on {specific feature or recent launch}. Our customers consistently ask for observability context alongside their service docs and API specs.

DevStack is a developer portal used by 1,200+ engineering teams. We surface service ownership, docs, and runbooks — but developers want health metrics and alerts inline too. Your data would be a natural fit.

Open to a 30-minute call to explore an integration partnership?

Best,
{sender_name}
```
