# Email Guidelines

## Structure
- **Subject**: under 8 words, lowercase, specific to recipient's situation (e.g., "your observability stack" not "Metricflow Analytics Platform Overview")
- **Opening**: reference something specific about them — a job posting, blog post, funding round, or mutual connection. Never generic.
- **Body**: max 120 words for cold outreach, max 150 for follow-ups. If it looks long when you glance at it, cut more.
- **CTA**: one clear, low-friction ask. "Worth a 15-min look?" or "Curious if this resonates?" — not "Let's schedule a 45-minute deep dive."
- **Signature**: first name only in cold emails. No titles, no links, no company tagline. Keep it human.

## Timing
- Send during recipient's business hours (use timezone from their LinkedIn location)
- Best days: Tuesday through Thursday
- Best times: 9-11am in their timezone
- Avoid: Monday mornings (inbox overload), Friday afternoons (mentally checked out)
- Follow-ups: send at a different time of day than the original email

## Personalization Requirements
- Every cold email MUST reference at least one specific detail about the recipient or their company
- Acceptable sources: job postings, eng blog, LinkedIn posts, conference talks, funding announcements, tech stack signals
- Never send the same message to two people at the same company — even if they're in different departments
- Match formality to the recipient's communication style (check their LinkedIn posts and company culture)

## Subject Line Rules
- Lowercase (feels like a peer email, not marketing)
- No company name in subject of first cold email
- Reference their world, not yours: "metrics across your stack" not "Metricflow unified analytics"
- No punctuation at the end
- No emoji
- Follow-ups: always "Re: [original subject]" to maintain thread

## Threading
- Follow-ups go in the same thread (Re: original subject)
- New topics or new angles after a pause get new threads with new subjects
- Max 1 follow-up per thread before trying a different channel (LinkedIn)
- After a full sequence (1 cold + 1 follow-up + 1 LinkedIn), wait 30 days before re-engaging with a new angle

## Deliverability
- No links in the first cold email (triggers spam filters)
- No images or HTML formatting in cold emails — plain text only
- No attachments in cold outreach
- Links are okay in follow-ups and replies after the prospect has engaged
- Keep subject lines clean — no RE: FWD: tricks or [brackets]

## Email Hygiene
- Verify email addresses before sending (bounce rate above 5% damages sender reputation)
- Remove hard bounces immediately
- If someone replies asking to be removed, do it instantly and confirm
- Track opens and replies to identify what's working
