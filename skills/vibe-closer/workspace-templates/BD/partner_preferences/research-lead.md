# Lead Research Strategy

How to research a potential partner before outreach. Follow these steps in order, using available MCPs.

## Research Steps

1. **Profile enrichment** via `{{PROFILE_ENRICHMENT}}`:
   - Decision-maker's LinkedIn profile: role, seniority, partnership history
   - Recent posts and activity — what initiatives are they driving?
   - Their network overlap with yours

2. **Company research** via `{{WEBSITE_CRAWLING}}`:
   - What the company does, key products/services, target market
   - Existing partnerships and integrations — who do they already work with?
   - Recent news: product launches, expansions, strategic shifts
   - API/developer docs if relevant (integration feasibility)
   - Shared customer base signals

3. **ICP matching** — cross-reference findings with `profile/ipp.md`:
   - Which ideal partner profile does this match?
   - What's the mutual value proposition?
   - What integration or co-marketing opportunities exist?

4. **Warm path check** via `{{FETCH_RELATIONSHIPS}}`:
   - Mutual connections who could facilitate introductions
   - Shared customers who could validate the partnership angle
   - Industry peers who've partnered with them before

## Output

Summarize research as:
- **Key findings**: 3-5 bullet points about the partner's business and fit
- **Partnership angle**: specific mutual value proposition with evidence
- **Personalization hooks**: details to reference (e.g., "your new marketplace launch," "the integration you built with [similar company]")
- **Warm paths**: mutual connections or shared customers
- **Recommended approach**: which template to use, what angle to lead with
