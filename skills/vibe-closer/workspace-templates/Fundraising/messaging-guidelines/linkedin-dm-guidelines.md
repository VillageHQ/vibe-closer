# LinkedIn DM Guidelines

LinkedIn is a secondary channel for investor outreach — use it when you have no email, or when the investor is active on LinkedIn but hasn't replied to your email.

## Structure
- **Max 100 words** — LinkedIn DMs should be shorter and more conversational than email
- **No subject line needed** — This is a chat, not email
- **Open with context** — Why you're reaching out to THIS person specifically
- **One clear ask** — Usually a meeting or conversation

## When to Use LinkedIn
1. **No email available**: When you can't find the investor's email and LinkedIn is your only option
2. **Email follow-up**: After 1 week with no email reply, send a LinkedIn message as a secondary touch
3. **Active on platform**: When the investor posts regularly and is clearly engaged on LinkedIn
4. **Relationship building**: When you want to comment on their posts or reference their recent activity

## Do
- **Reference something specific**: A recent post, article they shared, or portfolio company announcement
- **Keep it short**: 2-3 sentences for the initial message. You're not pitching yet; you're starting a conversation
- **Ask a genuine question**: "What's your take on AI copilots for enterprise workflows?" is better than a pitch
- **Be conversational**: Use natural language. This is a DM, not a formal email.
- **Show you know their work**: Reference a specific portfolio company or thesis point
- **Lead with the interesting part**: "$180K ARR, zero churn" grabs attention faster than "I'm a founder building an AI company"

## Don't
- **Send connection request + pitch in the same message**: Connect first, then message after they accept
- **Copy-paste email into LinkedIn**: LinkedIn conversations should feel different — lighter, more conversational
- **Use LinkedIn for long-form pitching**: If they're interested, move to email or call
- **Send unsolicited attachments**: Avoid sharing files in LinkedIn DMs
- **Over-explain**: The goal is a meeting. Keep them curious.
- **Use InMail for investors who accept connections**: InMail feels spammy. Connect first, then DM.

## Message Templates

### Initial LinkedIn Outreach (No Warm Intro)

```
Hi {{first_name}} — saw your recent post on {{topic}}, and it resonated with what we're building at Lumina AI. We're helping support teams resolve tickets 60% faster with an AI copilot — $180K ARR and growing 3x MoM.

Would love to chat about how you're thinking about AI in enterprise workflows.

Priya
```

### LinkedIn Follow-Up (After Email)

```
Hi {{first_name}} — following up on the email I sent last week about Lumina AI. We just hit a new milestone ({{recent_traction_update}}) and your thesis on {{topic}} keeps coming to mind.

Curious to get your take — open to a quick call?

Priya
```

### Connection Request Note

```
Hi {{first_name}} — I admire your work at {{firm_name}}, especially your investments in the AI/SaaS space. Building an AI copilot for support teams at Lumina AI ($180K ARR, 28 customers). Would love to connect.
```

### Responding to an Investor's Post (Before DM)

```
Great point about {{topic}}, {{first_name}}. We're seeing this firsthand at Lumina AI — our customers' support agents resolve tickets 60% faster when they have an AI copilot surfacing answers in real-time. The data flywheel effect you mentioned is exactly what drives our retention (135% NRR).
```

## LinkedIn Post Engagement (Optional)

Use this to warm up before reaching out via DM or email:
- Comment thoughtfully on their recent post with a relevant data point from Lumina AI's experience
- Share their post with your own perspective on AI in customer support
- Tag them in posts relevant to their thesis — but only if your comment adds genuine value

**Goal**: Get on their radar before you pitch, so your DM or email feels less cold.

## Response Expectations

- **First message response time**: 3-7 days (LinkedIn can be slower than email)
- **If no response to DM**: Try email instead, or have mutual connection send intro
- **If they engage on your posts**: They're interested. Time to reach out directly
- **If they view your profile**: They're curious. Send a thoughtful DM within 24 hours
