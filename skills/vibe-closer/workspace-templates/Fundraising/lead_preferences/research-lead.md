# Lead Research Strategy

How to research an investor before outreach. Follow these steps in order, using available MCPs.

## Research Steps

1. **Profile enrichment** via `{{PROFILE_ENRICHMENT}}`:
   - Partner's LinkedIn profile: background, investment focus, board seats
   - Recent posts and activity — what themes are they writing about?
   - Prior career: operator experience, domain expertise, stage preference

2. **Firm research** via `{{WEBSITE_CRAWLING}}`:
   - Investment thesis: stage, check size, sector focus
   - Portfolio companies — any overlap or adjacency to your space?
   - Recent fund announcements (new fund = actively deploying)
   - Blog posts or podcasts by partners about market trends
   - Any portfolio conflicts with your company?

3. **ICP matching** — cross-reference findings with `profile/icps.md`:
   - Which investor profile does this person match?
   - What about your company aligns with their thesis?
   - What proof points would resonate (traction, team, market size)?

4. **Warm path check** via `{{FETCH_RELATIONSHIPS}}`:
   - Mutual connections — especially other founders in their portfolio
   - Shared advisors, board members, or angel investors
   - Alumni networks or community overlap

## Output

Summarize research as:
- **Key findings**: 3-5 bullet points about the investor's focus and fit
- **Thesis alignment**: specific evidence that your company matches their investment criteria
- **Personalization hooks**: details to reference (e.g., "your recent post about vertical SaaS," "your investment in [similar company]")
- **Warm paths**: mutual connections ranked by intro strength
- **Recommended approach**: warm intro vs. cold, which template, what angle
