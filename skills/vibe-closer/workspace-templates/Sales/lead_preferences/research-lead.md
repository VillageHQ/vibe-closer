# Lead Research Strategy

How to research a new lead before outreach. Follow these steps in order, using available MCPs.

## Research Steps

1. **Profile enrichment** via `{{PROFILE_ENRICHMENT}}`:
   - Full LinkedIn profile: role, seniority, tenure, career trajectory
   - Recent posts and activity — what topics do they care about?
   - Shared connections for social proof

2. **Company research** via `{{WEBSITE_CRAWLING}}`:
   - What the company does, key products/services
   - Recent news: funding, product launches, expansions, leadership changes
   - Tech stack signals from job postings, blog, or documentation
   - Size, growth trajectory, and market position

3. **ICP matching** — cross-reference findings with `profile/icps.md`:
   - Which ICP does this lead match?
   - Which specific pain points are most likely relevant?
   - What proof points or case studies would resonate?

4. **Warm path check** via `{{FETCH_RELATIONSHIPS}}`:
   - Mutual connections who could provide intros or social proof
   - Shared communities, events, or affiliations

## Output

Summarize research as:
- **Key findings**: 3-5 bullet points of the most relevant discoveries
- **Personalization hooks**: specific details to reference in outreach (e.g., "just raised Series C," "posted about observability costs last week")
- **Pain point match**: which ICP pain points are most likely, with evidence
- **Warm paths**: any mutual connections or shared context
- **Recommended approach**: which template to use, what angle to lead with
