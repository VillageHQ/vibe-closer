# Goals

## Primary Goal

Book 15 qualified demos per month with mid-market SaaS companies (Series B-D, 200-1000 employees) for Metricflow's analytics infrastructure platform.

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Qualified demos booked | 15/month | Calendar invites with confirmed ICP-fit prospects |
| Outbound reply rate | >12% | Replies / total cold emails sent |
| Reply-to-meeting conversion | >35% | Meetings booked / positive replies |
| Pipeline value generated | $150K/month | Sum of estimated ACV for new opportunities |
| Average deal cycle | <45 days | First touch to closed-won |
| Email deliverability | >95% | Delivered / sent (monitor bounces weekly) |
| LinkedIn connection acceptance | >40% | Accepted / sent connection requests |

## Timeline

### Q1 — Foundation & Iteration
- Launch outbound sequences targeting ICP 1 (VP/Head of Engineering at SaaS)
- Test 3 subject line variants per ICP, identify top performer
- Establish baseline metrics across all channels
- Goal: 10 demos booked

### Q2 — Scale & Expand
- Add ICP 2 (Data/Analytics Lead at Fintech) to active sequences
- Scale to 15 demos/month run rate
- Implement warm intro pipeline via mutual connections
- Begin tracking objection patterns to refine messaging

### Q3 — Optimize & Systematize
- Achieve consistent 15+ demos/month
- Reduce time-to-reply by refining personalization hooks
- Build repeatable playbook from top-performing sequences
- Target 20% of demos from warm intros / inbound referrals

### Q4 — Expand Upmarket
- Test enterprise segment (1000-5000 employees) as ICP 3
- Increase pipeline value target to $250K/month
- Introduce multi-threaded outreach (reach 2+ stakeholders per account)
