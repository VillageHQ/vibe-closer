# Lead Discovery

## Sources to Check (in priority order)

### 1. Portfolio Referrals
- Ask portfolio founders: "Who's building something impressive in your space?"
- Check portfolio company Slack channels for mentions of interesting startups
- Review portfolio founders' angel investments and advisor roles

### 2. Meeting Notes
- Scan {{NOTETAKER}} for startup mentions from recent conversations
- Look for "you should talk to..." moments in partner meetings
- Check LP conversations for founder introductions

### 3. Network & Relationships
- Query {{FETCH_RELATIONSHIPS}} for connections to target founders
- Map co-investor networks — who are other seed investors backing?
- Check advisor and board member networks for warm paths

### 4. Market Signals
- Product Hunt launches in thesis areas
- Y Combinator / accelerator demo days
- Hacker News "Show HN" posts with traction
- Twitter/X founder threads about building in public
- AngelList / Wellfound new company listings
- Crunchbase funding alerts in target sectors

### 5. Inbound
- Check {{EMAIL_INBOX}} for founder cold emails and deck submissions
- Review LinkedIn messages from founders
- Conference and event introductions

## Qualification Criteria

### Must-Have
- Fits thesis (AI-native B2B SaaS, technical infrastructure, or vertical AI)
- Technical co-founder on the team
- Pre-Seed or Seed stage ($500K-$2M round)
- TAM > $1B

### Scoring (8-point scale)
| Signal | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Strong founder-market fit (domain expertise + technical chops) | 2 |
| Early revenue or strong design partner traction | 2 |
| Warm intro from trusted source (portfolio founder, co-investor) | 1 |
| AI is core architecture, not a feature | 1 |
| Clear competitive moat (proprietary data, novel approach) | 1 |
| Capital efficient (<$100K/month burn pre-revenue) | 1 |

### Priority Tiers
- **High (6-8)**: Take the meeting within 48 hours
- **Medium (4-5)**: Schedule within the week
- **Low (2-3)**: Add to pipeline, revisit when stronger signals emerge

### Disqualifiers
- No technical co-founder
- Round size >$5M (outside mandate)
- Consumer, crypto, hardware, biotech
- Founder can't articulate why AI is necessary
- Crowded market with well-funded incumbents already
