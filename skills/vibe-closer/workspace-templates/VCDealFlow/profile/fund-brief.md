# Fund Brief

## Fund Name
Horizon Ventures

## One-Liner
Early-stage fund investing $500K-$2M in B2B SaaS companies reinventing enterprise workflows with AI.

## Investment Thesis
We invest in technical founders building AI-native B2B SaaS products that replace legacy enterprise workflows. We look for companies where AI isn't a feature — it's the core architecture. Our sweet spot is the transition from early traction ($100K-$1M ARR) to product-market fit.

## Fund Details
- **Stage**: Pre-Seed and Seed
- **Check Size**: $500K - $2M
- **Follow-On**: Reserve 50% for follow-on in top portfolio companies
- **Fund Size**: $50M Fund II
- **Decision Timeline**: 2-4 weeks from first meeting to term sheet

## Portfolio Highlights
- DataCortex — AI-powered data pipeline orchestration ($3M ARR, Series A)
- SpectrumOps — Automated compliance monitoring for fintech ($1.2M ARR, Seed)
- NeuralDocs — AI document processing for legal teams ($800K ARR, Seed)
- BuildAI — Code generation for enterprise integration ($500K ARR, Pre-Seed)

## What We Bring Beyond Capital
- Deep B2B SaaS operating experience (partner was VP Eng at Salesforce, VP Product at Datadog)
- Active portfolio network — founders help each other with hiring, GTM, and technical architecture
- Enterprise customer introductions from our LP base (Fortune 500 CIOs)
- Recruiting support through our talent network (200+ vetted senior engineers)

## What We Don't Do
- Consumer, crypto, hardware, biotech
- Companies with <$50K ARR unless exceptional team + market
- Rounds above $5M (outside our check size range)
- Companies where AI is a bolt-on feature, not core architecture
