# LinkedIn DM Guidelines

## When to Use LinkedIn
- Secondary channel after email no-reply (3-7 days)
- When you don't have the founder's email
- To engage with their content before cold outreach (comment on posts first)

## Connection Request
- Max 60 words
- Reference something specific about their company or a recent post
- Don't pitch — just connect
- Example: "Hi {first_name} — I invest in {space} at Horizon. {reference their product or recent news}. Would love to connect."

## First DM (after accepted)
- Max 80 words, conversational tone
- Reference their company specifically — show you've done homework
- One clear ask: "Open to a quick chat?"
- Don't copy-paste your email into LinkedIn
- Don't attach your fund deck

## Engagement Strategy
- Follow target founders before reaching out
- Like and comment on their posts with genuine insights (not "Great post!")
- Share relevant content about their market
- Build visibility before the ask

## Frequency
- Max 1 connection request + 1 follow-up DM per founder
- If no response after DM follow-up, do not message again on LinkedIn
- Wait 30+ days before re-engaging with a new angle
