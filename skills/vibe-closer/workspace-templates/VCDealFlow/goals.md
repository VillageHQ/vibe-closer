# Goals

## Primary Goal
Source and close 2-3 investments per quarter that align with our thesis.

## Success Metrics
- 15+ new startup conversations per month
- 8-10 first meetings per month
- 3-5 partner meetings per month
- 2-3 term sheets per quarter
- Average time from first meeting to term sheet: 4-6 weeks

## Timeline
- Month 1: Build pipeline of 50+ qualified startups
- Month 2: Steady-state outreach producing 8-10 meetings/month
- Month 3: First 1-2 deals moving to due diligence
- Quarter 2+: Consistent deal flow with 2-3 closings per quarter

## Key Principles
- Quality over quantity — 5 deep-conviction meetings beat 20 surface-level ones
- Founders talk to each other — every interaction shapes your reputation
- Speed is a competitive advantage — respond to warm intros within 24 hours
- Thesis discipline — don't waste founders' time on deals outside your mandate
