# Goals

## Primary Goal
Hire 4 senior engineers (2 backend, 1 frontend, 1 ML) by end of Q2 2026 to support Series B product expansion.

## Success Metrics
- **Pipeline volume**: 30 qualified candidates in active pipeline at any time
- **Outreach velocity**: 15 new candidates contacted per week
- **Response rate**: >25% response rate on outreach (industry avg is 15-20%)
- **Interview conversion**: Convert 40% of responses to first interviews
- **Offer acceptance**: >75% offer acceptance rate
- **Time to hire**: <45 days from first contact to signed offer

## Timeline
- March 2026: Launch outreach for Backend Engineer #1 and Frontend Engineer
- April 2026: Launch ML Engineer search, close Backend #1
- May 2026: Close Frontend and ML roles, launch Backend #2
- June 2026: Close Backend #2, all roles filled

## Review Cadence
- Weekly: Review pipeline health, response rates, interview pipeline
- Bi-weekly: Run /learn to optimize outreach messaging
- Monthly: Assess if candidate profiles need adjustment based on interview feedback
