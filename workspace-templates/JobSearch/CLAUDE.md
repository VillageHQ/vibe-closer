## How It Works

This workspace helps you systematically reach out to target companies and hiring managers. The typical flow:

1. **Research**: Identify target companies and roles that match your goals
2. **Find Contact**: Locate hiring managers, recruiters, or internal connections
3. **Outreach**: Send personalized messages showing you've done your homework
4. **Follow-up**: Maintain momentum with strategic follow-ups
5. **Interview Prep**: Move successful contacts toward interviews

The workflow planner defines stages and rules for progression. Templates ensure consistent, personalized messaging. Your candidate brief and target company profiles keep everything aligned.
