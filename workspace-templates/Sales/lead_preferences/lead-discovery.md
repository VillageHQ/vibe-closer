# Lead Discovery

## Sources to Check

1. **Meeting notes** via `{{NOTETAKER}}`:
   - Fetch conversations from the last 2 weeks
   - Look for mentions of potential customers, referrals, or intro requests

2. **Email inbox** via `{{EMAIL_INBOX}}`:
   - Scan inbound emails from the last 2 weeks
   - Look for: demo requests, product questions, intro emails, warm referrals

3. **Relationships** via `{{FETCH_RELATIONSHIPS}}`:
   - Check for new connections relevant to ICPs

## Qualification Criteria

A lead qualifies if:
- Matches at least one ICP in `profile/icps.md`
- Has a valid contact method (email or LinkedIn)
- Is not already tracked in `{{CRM_TRACKER}}`

## Output

For each discovered lead, provide:
- Contact name and email
- Company and domain
- Source (where discovered)
- ICP match (which profile and why)
- Recommended initial action
