# Email Templates

## Cold Email

**Subject**: [Short, specific, no clickbait]

**Body**:
```
Hi {{first_name}},

[1 sentence about them or their company — show you did homework]

[1-2 sentences about what you do and why it's relevant to them]

[Clear CTA — question or specific ask]

Best,
{{sender_name}}
```

## Follow-Up (No Reply)

**Subject**: Re: [original subject]

**Body**:
```
Hi {{first_name}},

[Brief reference to previous email]

[New angle or additional value — don't just repeat yourself]

[Lighter CTA]

{{sender_name}}
```

## Warm Intro Request

**Subject**: [Name] suggested I reach out

**Body**:
```
Hi {{first_name}},

{{mutual_connection}} mentioned you might be a great person to connect with about [topic].

[Brief context on what you do]

Would you be open to a quick chat?

{{sender_name}}
```

## Post-Meeting Follow-Up

**Subject**: Great chatting — [topic]

**Body**:
```
Hi {{first_name}},

Thanks for the conversation today. Key takeaways:
- [Action item 1]
- [Action item 2]

[Next step with specific date/time]

{{sender_name}}
```
