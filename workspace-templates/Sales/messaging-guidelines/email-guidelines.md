# Email Guidelines

## Structure
- Subject: under 8 words, specific to recipient
- Opening: reference something specific about them (not generic)
- Body: max 150 words for cold outreach, max 200 for follow-ups
- CTA: one clear ask, easy to say yes to
- Signature: name only, no titles or links in cold emails

## Timing
- Send during recipient's business hours (use timezone from profile)
- Best days: Tuesday through Thursday
- Avoid: Monday mornings, Friday afternoons

## Personalization Requirements
- Every cold email must reference at least one specific detail about the recipient or their company
- Never send the same message to two people at the same company
- Match formality level to recipient's communication style (check their LinkedIn posts)

## Threading
- Follow-ups go in the same thread (Re: original subject)
- New topics get new threads
- Max 2 follow-ups per thread before trying a new angle
