# Company Brief — Employer Brand

## What We Do
Buildkraft is a developer infrastructure company that helps engineering teams ship code 10x faster by automating CI/CD pipelines, test environments, and deployment workflows. Think of us as the operating system for modern software delivery.

## Why Engineers Join Us
- **Hard problems**: We're building distributed systems that handle 2M+ deployments/day. Real infra challenges, not CRUD apps.
- **Impact**: Every engineer ships to production in their first week. Small team (28 people), so every person's work is visible.
- **Technical culture**: 40% of time is self-directed. We do RFCs for major decisions, and engineers own their systems end-to-end.
- **Compensation**: Top-of-market salary + meaningful equity (0.1-0.5% for senior hires). We benchmark against Series C companies.

## Traction
- $4.2M ARR, growing 3x YoY
- 340+ engineering teams using the platform
- 99.97% uptime over the last 12 months
- Raised $18M Series A from Sequoia and a16z

## Team
- **CEO**: Former Stripe eng lead, built their deployment infra
- **CTO**: Ex-Google, led the Cloud Build team
- **Eng team**: 14 engineers from Stripe, Vercel, Datadog, and Cloudflare

## Culture
- Remote-first, async by default, with quarterly team onsites
- We write more than we meet — docs and RFCs over standups
- No on-call rotation for non-infra roles (dedicated SRE team)
