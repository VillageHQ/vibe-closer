## Forwardable Blurb

This is the email someone sends ON YOUR BEHALF to an investor. Keep it under 80 words. Make it easy to forward.

**Subject**: {{mutual_connection}} — intro to Priya at Lumina AI

**Body**:
```
Hi {{investor_first_name}},

Wanted to connect you with Priya Sharma, founder of Lumina AI. They've built an AI copilot for customer support teams — sits inside Zendesk/Intercom and helps agents resolve tickets 60% faster. $180K ARR, 28 customers, zero churn, growing 3x month-over-month. Priya was the engineering lead at Zendesk who built their ML ticket routing.

They're raising a $4M Seed and I thought you'd be a great fit given {{specific_thesis_or_portfolio_reason}}.

I'll let you two take it from here.

{{mutual_connection}}
```
