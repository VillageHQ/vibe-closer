# Goals

## Primary Goal

Close a **$4M Seed round** for Lumina AI by **June 2026**.

Lumina AI is raising its Seed round to scale go-to-market, expand the engineering team, and hit $1M ARR — positioning for a strong Series A in 12-18 months.

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Pipeline volume | 60 qualified investors in active pipeline |
| Warm intro rate | >70% of outreach goes through warm introductions |
| First call conversion | 40% of first meetings advance to partner meeting |
| Term sheet target | 2-3 term sheets to create competitive dynamics |
| Average time to close | <90 days from first outreach to signed term sheet |
| Weekly meeting pace | 8-10 investor meetings per week during peak outreach |

## Timeline

| Phase | Weeks | Dates | Activities |
|-------|-------|-------|------------|
| **Build List** | Week 1-2 | Mar 16 - Mar 29 | Map warm paths, qualify 60 investors, prep data room, finalize forwardable blurb |
| **Peak Outreach** | Week 3-6 | Mar 30 - Apr 26 | Launch warm intros (batch 1: top 20 investors), run 8-10 meetings/week, refine pitch based on feedback |
| **Partner Meetings** | Week 6-10 | Apr 27 - Jun 1 | Deep-dive sessions with interested firms, reference calls, data room sharing, due diligence support |
| **Close** | Week 10-12 | Jun 1 - Jun 15 | Negotiate term sheets, select lead investor, close round, wire funds |

## Target Allocation

| Investor Type | Target Amount | Notes |
|---------------|--------------|-------|
| Lead VC (1 firm) | $2.0M - $2.5M | Seed-stage AI/SaaS fund; board seat likely |
| Follow-on VCs (1-2 firms) | $1.0M - $1.5M | Complementary funds with CX or AI thesis |
| Strategic Angels | $500K - $750K | Former CX operators (Zendesk, Intercom, Freshdesk alumni), 5-8 angels at $50K-$150K each |
| Angel Syndicates | $250K - $500K | Operator-led syndicates with SaaS or AI focus |

## Key Investor Criteria

- **AI/SaaS thesis**: Must have a stated thesis or portfolio track record in applied AI or B2B SaaS
- **Seed-stage focus**: Actively writing $500K-$2.5M checks at Seed
- **CX/support domain knowledge preferred**: Investors who understand customer support workflows, contact center economics, or CX platforms get priority
- **Founder-friendly terms**: Looking for a lead who offers fair valuation, standard protective provisions, and meaningful post-investment support
- **Geographic flexibility**: Open to US-based or global investors; no geographic restriction
- **No portfolio conflicts**: Cannot have existing investments in direct competitors (AI agent builders for support: Forethought, Thankful, Cognigy CX)
- **Value-add beyond capital**: Preference for investors who can help with enterprise sales intros, CX industry connections, or technical recruiting
