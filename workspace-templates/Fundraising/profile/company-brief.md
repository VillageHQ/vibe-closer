## One-Liner

Lumina AI is an AI copilot for customer support teams that reduces resolution time by 60% while improving CSAT scores.

## Problem

Customer support teams are drowning. Ticket volumes grow 15-20% annually while hiring can't keep up. The average support agent handles 40+ tickets/day across multiple tools, spending 35% of their time just searching for answers across knowledge bases, past tickets, and internal docs.

- **Who faces this**: Mid-market and enterprise companies with 10-200 person support teams
- **The cost**: Average fully-loaded cost per ticket is $15-25. A 100-person support org spends $8-12M/year, with 30-40% of that cost going to repetitive, searchable tasks
- **Why unsolved**: Legacy solutions (chatbots, macro tools, basic automation) handle only simple FAQs. They break on complex, multi-step tickets that require reasoning across multiple data sources. LLMs changed what's possible, but existing support platforms haven't rebuilt their stack around them

## Solution

Lumina AI sits alongside agents in their existing helpdesk (Zendesk, Intercom, Freshdesk). When a ticket comes in, Lumina instantly:

1. **Surfaces the right answer** by reasoning across knowledge bases, past tickets, product docs, and internal wikis
2. **Drafts a response** in the company's voice and tone, with citations
3. **Handles routine tickets autonomously** when confidence is high (currently 28% of tickets fully auto-resolved)

What makes us different:
- **Retrieval-augmented generation tuned for support**: Our RAG pipeline is purpose-built for support workflows — handling multi-turn conversations, product-specific jargon, and escalation logic
- **Learns from agent corrections**: Every time an agent edits a suggested response, the model improves. Customers see measurable quality gains week-over-week
- **Works with existing tools**: No rip-and-replace. Deploys as a browser extension + API integration in under 2 hours
